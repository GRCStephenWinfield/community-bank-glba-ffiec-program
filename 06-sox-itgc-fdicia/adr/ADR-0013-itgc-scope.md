## Context
SOX §404 requires ITGCs that support ICFR over the financially-significant systems. The control set must be sufficient but not excessive.

## Decision
Scope **48 key IT general controls** across the four domains (Access to Programs & Data, Program Changes, Program Development/SDLC, Computer Operations) over the 6 significant systems, using a top-down risk-based approach.

## Alternatives Considered
**Test hundreds of controls** — rejected: disproportionate. **Minimal controls** — rejected: insufficient ICFR support.

## Consequences
A focused, defensible ITGC population; efficient testing; clean external-auditor reliance.
