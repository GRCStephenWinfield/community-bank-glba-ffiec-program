## What this phase is

Because Cornerstone Bank is a subsidiary of a **publicly traded holding company (Cornerstone Bancorp, Nasdaq: CCBK)**, it is subject to **SOX §404** — and at over $1B in assets, to **FDICIA Part 363**. Both require assurance over **Internal Control over Financial Reporting (ICFR)**. This phase delivers the **IT General Controls (ITGC)** that underpin ICFR: **48 key controls** across the four ITGC domains — Access to Programs & Data, Program Changes, Program Development/SDLC, and Computer Operations — over the **6 financially-significant systems**, with **SOC 1 Type II reliance on Meridian** for the outsourced core. It runs the full test of design and operating effectiveness, evaluates deficiencies, remediates them, and lands the management assertion.
