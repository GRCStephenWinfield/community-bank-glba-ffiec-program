## Context
IT examinations involve large, sensitive (NPI) evidence flows; uncontrolled responses create errors and disclosure risk.

## Decision
Designate a **single point of contact** (CISO-led) to manage the first-day letter, evidence index, and examiner requests, with controlled NPI handling.

## Alternatives Considered
**Ad hoc responses by many staff** — rejected: inconsistent; disclosure risk.

## Consequences
Controlled, accurate, timely evidence flow; a smoother Satisfactory exam.
