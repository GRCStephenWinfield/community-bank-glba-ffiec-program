Program Summary

An end-to-end GLBA / FFIEC / SOX program, delivered in ~12 months

Cornerstone Community Bank built its information security and compliance program across nine structured phases — from a GLBA §501(b) risk assessment (42 risks) and a board-approved Written Information Security Program, through a NIST CSF 2.0 maturity assessment and SOX ITGC testing, to independent validation. The program passed its FFIEC IT examination with a Satisfactory rating (URSIT composite "2"), earned an unqualified SOX 404(b) opinion (0 material weaknesses), remediated every penetration-test finding, and now operates at a Low-to-Moderate residual risk posture.
