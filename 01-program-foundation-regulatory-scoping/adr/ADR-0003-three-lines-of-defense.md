## Context
Examiners expect clear separation between control ownership, risk oversight, and independent assurance.

## Decision
Adopt the **three-lines-of-defense** model: business/IT owns controls (1st line), Risk & Compliance provide oversight (2nd line), Internal Audit provides independent assurance (3rd line), with the Audit Committee at the apex.

## Alternatives Considered
**Two-line model** — rejected: blurs assurance independence. **No formal model** — rejected: examiner finding risk.

## Consequences
Clear accountability; independent assurance preserved; aligns to FFIEC and SOX expectations.
