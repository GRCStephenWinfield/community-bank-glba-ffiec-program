## Context
GLBA §501(b) and the Interagency Guidelines require a written, board-overseen information security program. Cornerstone's controls existed but were not consolidated under a single governed program.

## Decision
Charter a **formal Information Security & Compliance Program** owned by the CISO, overseen by the Board Audit Committee, covering GLBA, FFIEC, NIST CSF 2.0, SOX ITGC, and FDICIA.

## Alternatives Considered
**Keep controls decentralized** — rejected: no single accountable owner; examiner risk. **Outsource wholesale** — rejected: accountability cannot be outsourced.

## Consequences
Single accountable owner; board oversight satisfied; a defensible program for examination.
