## What this phase is

This phase establishes the **foundation** of Cornerstone's information security and compliance program. It profiles the Bank, maps the regulators and the full obligation set (GLBA §501(b), Regulation P, the FFIEC IT Examination Handbook, NIST CSF 2.0, SOX §404 ITGC, FDICIA Part 363, and the 36-hour incident-notification rule), charters the program, and defines governance, roles, scope, the roadmap, and the regulatory obligations calendar. Everything downstream — the risk assessment, the control program, the FFIEC/NIST assessment, SOX ITGC, and board reporting — sits on this foundation.
