## Context
Maturity targets should be commensurate with inherent risk. Cornerstone's FFIEC inherent risk is overall Moderate.

## Decision
Set the **target maturity profile to Intermediate** across the CSF 2.0 functions, right-sizing investment to a Moderate inherent-risk institution.

## Alternatives Considered
**Target Advanced everywhere** — rejected: disproportionate cost. **Stay at Evolving** — rejected: below examiner expectation for the risk.

## Consequences
A proportionate target; 28 gaps to close; a fundable roadmap.
