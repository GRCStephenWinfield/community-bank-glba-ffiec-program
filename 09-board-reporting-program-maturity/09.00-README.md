## What this phase is

This is the **capstone**. It rolls the whole nine-phase program up to the **Board and executives**, delivers the **statutory annual GLBA §501(b) report** the Interagency Guidelines require, scores program maturity, presents the executive KPI/KRI dashboard and residual-risk posture, consolidates the regulatory exam and audit outcomes, and sets the forward continuous-improvement roadmap — then closes the portfolio to steady-state operation. The verdict: a program that is **compliant and well-managed**, with a **Satisfactory FFIEC IT exam (URSIT-2)**, an **unqualified SOX 404(b) opinion**, all High risks treated, and **residual risk Low-to-Moderate**.
