## Context
GLBA §501(b) and the Interagency Guidelines require a **report to the board at least annually** on the information security program.

## Decision
Establish an **annual GLBA report to the Board** (program status, risk assessment, service-provider oversight, testing, incidents, recommendations) with a standing calendar slot and a defined template.

## Alternatives Considered
**Ad hoc reporting** — rejected: risks missing the statutory obligation. **Management-only reporting** — rejected: board oversight required.

## Consequences
Statutory obligation satisfied; documented board oversight; a repeatable template.
