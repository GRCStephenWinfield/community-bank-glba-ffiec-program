## Context
The Board and executives need concise, forward-looking indicators of security & compliance health between annual reports.

## Decision
Adopt an **executive KPI/KRI scorecard** (patch SLA, MFA coverage, access reviews, training, vendor reviews, phishing rate, incidents, findings, ICFR) reported quarterly with RAG thresholds.

## Alternatives Considered
**Narrative-only updates** — rejected: not measurable. **Operational metrics only** — rejected: not board-appropriate.

## Consequences
Measurable oversight; early warning via KRIs; sustained maturity focus.
