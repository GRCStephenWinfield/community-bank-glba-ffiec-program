## Context
Risk ratings must be consistent and transparent across dozens of risks and multiple contributors.

## Decision
Use a **5×5 likelihood × impact matrix** with defined scale anchors and High/Moderate/Low thresholds, calibrated in a management risk committee.

## Alternatives Considered
**3×3 matrix** — rejected: too coarse. **Ad hoc High/Med/Low** — rejected: inconsistent.

## Consequences
Consistent ratings; the register reconciles to 8 High / 18 Moderate / 16 Low.
