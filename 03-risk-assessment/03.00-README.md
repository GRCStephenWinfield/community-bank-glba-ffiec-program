## What this phase is

GLBA §501(b) and the Interagency Guidelines require the Bank to **identify reasonably foreseeable internal and external threats** to the security, confidentiality, and integrity of customer NPI, and to assess the sufficiency of controls in place. This phase delivers that statutory risk assessment: a NIST SP 800-30-aligned methodology, the threat landscape, the NPI-specific threat analysis across the **22 NPI systems**, a vulnerability assessment, the **FFIEC inherent-risk profile (overall Moderate)**, and the **risk register — 42 risks rated 8 High, 18 Moderate, 16 Low** — closing with the formal risk-assessment report presented to the Board. The 8 High risks and the preliminary control gaps drive the control program (Phase 04) and the FFIEC/NIST CSF 2.0 maturity assessment (Phase 05).
