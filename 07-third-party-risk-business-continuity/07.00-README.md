## What this phase is

A community bank that outsources its core runs on its vendors — so this phase governs them, and prepares for the day something breaks. It stands up the **Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) program** aligned to the **2023 Interagency Guidance on Third-Party Relationships** across **85 vendors (12 critical/high)**, with **Meridian under enhanced oversight** given concentration risk. It then builds the resilience layer: a **business continuity plan** with RTO/RPO targets, a **tested disaster-recovery** capability, a full **incident-response plan**, an **IR tabletop** (ransomware / NPI breach), and the **36-hour regulatory notification runbook**. Together these directly close the Detect/Respond/Recover maturity gaps from Phase 05.
