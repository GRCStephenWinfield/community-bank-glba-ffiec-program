## Context
The core banking/GL and digital channels are outsourced to Meridian, creating concentration risk on a single provider.

## Decision
Apply **enhanced oversight** to Meridian (SOC reliance, SLA governance, BCP/DR alignment, continuous monitoring) and maintain a documented **exit strategy** to mitigate concentration risk.

## Alternatives Considered
**Standard oversight** — rejected: understates concentration risk. **Re-insource the core** — rejected: impractical at this scale.

## Consequences
Concentration risk managed; continuity assured; a credible exit path.
