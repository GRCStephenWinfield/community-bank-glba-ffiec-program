## Context
Cornerstone relies on 85 third parties, including for the core. Oversight effort must be proportionate to each vendor's risk.

## Decision
Adopt a **4-tier vendor risk model** (Critical/High/Moderate/Low) based on NPI access, operational criticality, and financial-reporting relevance, driving diligence depth and reassessment cadence per the 2023 Interagency Guidance.

## Alternatives Considered
**Treat all vendors equally** — rejected: wasteful and dilutes focus. **Only assess the core** — rejected: leaves gaps.

## Consequences
Proportionate oversight; 12 critical/high vendors get the most scrutiny; defensible program.
