## What this phase is

You cannot protect — or assess the risk to — what you have not inventoried. This phase builds the **authoritative picture of what Cornerstone has and where its sensitive data lives**: the enterprise asset and system inventory (**140 systems**), a four-tier **data classification scheme**, an **NPI data map** across the **22 systems** that hold customer nonpublic personal information, the network architecture and segmentation, and identification of the **6 financially-significant systems** in scope for SOX ITGC. Everything the risk assessment (Phase 03) and the control program (Phase 04) do is scoped by what this phase catalogs.
